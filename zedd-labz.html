STAGE 07

ZEDD LABZ WEBSITE REDESIGN

★ MY ROLE

During my one-month internship at Zedd Labz, I had the role of UI/UX Designer, with primary responsibility of redesigning the Zedd Labz website.

★ PROTOTYPE DEMO
★ DESIGN BRIEF

Zedd Labz faced a weak online presence that hindered potential clients from learning about the company. I collaborated to redesign their website with objectives to elevate their brand, increase visibility, and enable effortless exploration of their services.

★ PROBLEMS
  • Outdated Design: Previous design appeared outdated, undermining trust for first-time visitors.
  • Trust & Security: Outdated appearance raised concerns about website security and trustworthiness.
  • Poor Homepage Visibility: Key information was not presented clearly, hindering users from grasping key details.
  • Inefficient Content Management: Difficulty updating content led to stale or outdated information.
★ GOALS
  • Modernise Design: Update overall design to eliminate the outdated appearance and instil trust.
  • Enhance Trust: Clear calls to action and visible security indicators.
  • Optimise Homepage: Redesign to prominently display key information.
  • Improve UX: Enhance navigation and ensure seamless interactions.
★ BEFORE & AFTER
★ USER RESEARCH

Conducted a survey among IT service professionals and in-depth interviews with 5 participants, collecting data from 120 survey responses.

★ COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Comprehensive analysis of several IT company websites yielded key takeaways:

  • Design Inspiration: Valuable insights into design elements and aesthetics.
  • Functional Enhancements: User-friendly functionalities and interactive features.
  • Content Strategy: Refined communication of key messages and offerings.
★ IDEATION

Zedd Labz website should include: engaging design, informative content, service showcase, project portfolio, client testimonials, visible contact info, social media integration, and lead generation opt-in forms.

★ SITEMAP

Site map organised to ensure a logical flow and easy navigation, establishing a clear hierarchy aligned with website goals.

★ USER FLOW

User flow mapping the intended journey from the landing page through to exploring services and completing desired tasks.

★ WIREFRAMES

Visual blueprints depicting placement of headers, navigation menus, content sections, and interactive features.

★ UI SCREENS

Some of the completed final UI screens showcasing the visual design and layout of key sections.

★ PROTOTYPE
★ IMPACT

In four weeks, designs had a profound impact on ongoing website development. Prioritising user-centric design, intuitive navigation and visual appeal, user engagement improved and a cohesive brand identity was established.

★ REFLECTION

My first UI/UX design internship was a valuable learning experience — practical skills in user research, wireframing, and interface design. I discovered the importance of collaboration and effective communication. The internship ignited my passion for continuous learning in UI/UX design.
