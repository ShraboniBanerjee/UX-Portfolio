STAGE 04

HOTEL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

★ COMPANY OVERVIEW

Adevu is a newly established IT startup that helps business owners bring their web presence into the modern age. Based in New Delhi, India and New York, US.

★ MY ROLE

I worked as a Frontend Software Engineer where my primary responsibility was developing the UI for a Hotel Management System from scratch.

★ HERO DEMO
★ BRIEF

I played a key role in developing a Hotel Management System from scratch using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and later React.js. I collaborated with a product manager, developers, testers, and designers. Adevu's goal was to provide hotel administrators with a comprehensive solution to streamline operations and enhance guest satisfaction.

★ PROBLEMS
  • Complex Requirements: Gathering requirements across hotels with varying processes required extensive research and collaboration.
  • UI Design: Balancing aesthetics, functionality and usability while considering diverse needs required iterative design.
  • Scalability: The initial HTML/CSS/JS stack required a transition to React.js as complexity grew.
  • Backend Integration: Coordinating frontend and backend synchronisation required close collaboration.
  • Testing & QA: Comprehensive testing strategy required across stability, reliability, and security.
  • Time Constraints: Developing from scratch within limited time required effective project management.
★ GOALS
  • Streamline Operations: Automate room reservations, check-ins/outs, housekeeping, inventory, billing, and reporting.
  • Enhance Guest Experience: Smooth check-ins, real-time availability, and personalised interactions.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive, visually appealing UI reducing training time.
  • Integration & Data Consistency: Seamless integration with PMS and payment gateways.
  • Scalability: Support multiple properties and evolving business requirements.
  • Security & Privacy: Secure authentication, data encryption, and access controls.
★ USER RESEARCH

User research was conducted to understand needs and pain points across room availability, guest preferences, housekeeping, billing, and communication.

★ COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

The competitive analysis assessed key players including Cloudbeds and MakeMyTrip to identify opportunities for differentiation and effective market positioning.

★ COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

After competitive analysis, Adevu positioned itself as a provider of choice through targeted features for smaller hotels relying on paper-based systems.

★ IDEATION

Primary objective: create an innovative solution addressing challenges faced by hotels on paper-based systems.

  • Centralized Preference Management: Centralised database for guest preferences.
  • Automated Notifications: Inform staff about guest preferences for personalised services.
  • Seamless Integration: Integrate with existing PMS and payment gateways.
  • Scalability & Flexibility: Adaptable to evolving hotel needs.
