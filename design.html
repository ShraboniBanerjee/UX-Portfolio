★ PROTOTYPE DEMO
★ PROBLEM STATEMENT

RestoGenius aimed to provide an affordable POS solution for small businesses in the Australian hospitality sector:

  • Affordability: Existing POS systems were costly for small cafes, bars, and franchises, impacting their ability to invest in streamlining technology.
  • Feature Suitability: Larger POS systems included unnecessary features, complicating usability and increasing costs without corresponding benefits.
★ USER RESEARCH

Analysed market trends and competitor offerings to identify gaps in affordable, feature-rich POS solutions.

Note: Due to time constraints, interviews were not conducted. The design process relied on information provided by the CEO and director to deliver the entire design within a tight deadline.

★ SOLUTION
  • Affordable Pricing Model: Subscription-based pricing tailored to small business financial capabilities at a competitive price point.
  • Streamlined UI: Intuitive interface simplifying order management, inventory tracking, and customer interactions.
  • Customisable Features: Modular features allowing businesses to customise the POS according to specific needs.
