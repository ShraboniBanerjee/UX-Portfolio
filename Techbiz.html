GEMSTONE ADMIN PANEL

★ MY ROLE

During my six-month internship as a Software Engineer and UI/UX Design intern at Techbiz Innovators, I contributed to the development of the Keystar Gemstone Admin Panel. My role involved research, UI design, prototyping, and frontend development to create an intuitive gemstone management tool for the internal team.

★ BRIEF

During my 6-month internship at Techbiz Innovators, an Australian IT startup, I contributed to the development of the Keystar Gemstone Admin Panel — a web-based application designed to streamline gemstone inventory management for Keystar, a renowned gemstone supplier.

As a Software Engineer and UI/UX Designer, I conducted research, designed the UI, created prototypes, and implemented frontend development to ensure an intuitive and efficient user experience for the internal team at Keystar.

★ PROBLEMS
  • Complex Data Management: The system had to handle vast amounts of gemstone data including type, carat weight, cut, clarity, color, and origin. Organizing this data effectively was crucial for accurate inventory tracking.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The Admin Panel needed to allow Keystar's internal team to easily add, edit, and search for gemstones with intuitive navigation and clear functionalities.
  • Security and Access Control: As the Admin Panel contained sensitive information, robust security measures and access control had to be implemented to protect data from unauthorized access.
★ GOALS
  • Streamlined Data Entry: A simplified and structured data entry system enabling the Keystar team to enter gemstone details efficiently, ensuring accuracy and reducing errors.
  • Advanced Search and Filtering: Robust search and filtering functionality allowing users to quickly locate specific gemstones based on various criteria, enhancing productivity.
  • Role-Based Access Control: A role-based access control system granting different levels of permissions to users based on their roles, ensuring sensitive data is only accessible to authorized personnel.
★ USER RESEARCH

To gain insights into target users' preferences, needs, and pain points, user research activities were conducted — including interviews, surveys, and usability testing with individuals who would be using the admin panel regularly. The research aimed to identify user workflows, pain points, and opportunities for improvement.

★ COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

A comprehensive competitive analysis was conducted to understand the landscape and identify opportunities for differentiation. The analysis focused on key competitors in the gemstone inventory management software market and evaluated their strengths and weaknesses.

★ IDEATION

Based on user research and competitive analysis, the following features were proposed:

  • User Experience: Highly intuitive and user-friendly interface to minimize learning curve and enhance ease of use.
  • Real-time Updates: Real-time inventory updates and notifications for accurate stock management and efficient order fulfillment.
  • Customization: Flexible customization options to cater to the specific needs of gemstone inventory management at Keystar.
  • Integration Capabilities: Seamless integration with CRM systems, e-commerce platforms, and other business systems.
  • Reporting and Analytics: Advanced reporting and analytics features to provide insights into inventory performance and trends.

After the ideation phase, the team approved the proposed features. Due to limited time, the focus shifted to creating the sitemap, user flow, wireframe, and UI design process — allowing for a streamlined workflow to deliver a well-structured product within the given timeframe.

Wireframes were created as a visual representation of the interface structure and functionality, outlining the placement of key elements such as navigation menus, content sections, forms, buttons, and interactive components.

★ FRONTEND DEVELOPMENT

The development team collectively decided to use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend development of the Keystar Gems project — chosen for versatility, widespread adoption, and compatibility with web standards.

★ DARK / LIGHT MODE

The dark-light mode feature added to Keystar Gems enhances the user experience by providing a visually appealing and comfortable viewing option. It improves accessibility and allows users to personalize their experience based on preferences.
